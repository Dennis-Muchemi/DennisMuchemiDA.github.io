Data Professionals Survey Insights

Dive deep into the world of data careers through this Power BI-driven survey analysis. This project encapsulates a holistic view of the data professional landscape, offering visualizations on the total survey participants, average age, and the relationship between job titles and corresponding salaries. Geographical insights reveal the diverse origins of survey takers, while a gender-based salary breakdown highlights disparities within the industry. The visualization extends its focus to happiness metrics, capturing sentiments on both work-life balance and salary satisfaction. In this project, I've leveraged my data analysis and visualization skills to provide a resource that not only showcases industry trends but also serves as a guide for individuals navigating their own data-centric careers.
