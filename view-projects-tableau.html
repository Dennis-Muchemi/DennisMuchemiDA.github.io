Seattle AirBnB Insights

This project is a visual journey through Seattle's Airbnb market unravelling key insights to empower hosts and property investors. It illuminates the distribution of listings based on bedroom count, highlights of average price per bedroom, and maps out pricing dynamics across different zip codes. By examining the flow of revenue throughout the year, actionable intelligence is offered to enhance hosting strategies.
